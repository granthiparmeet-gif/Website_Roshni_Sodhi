Q&A

Q. Who is a lactation professional?
A. A person trained in guiding a new mother in dealing with breastfeeding issues.

Q. What is the correct time to be educated about breastfeeding?
A. In Pregnancy preferably last trimester.

Q. What is the right time to consult a lactation professional?
A. First in pregnancy for education, then after delivery for initiation of breastfeeding, in first week post delivery for adequate latch, thereafter anytime in six months when milk supply is a concern or any pain in breast.

Q. Is breastfeeding painful?
A. Not at all, if done with correct technique.

Q. What is the one most important technique of breastfeeding a mother should learn?
A. Correct latch.

Q. What is the one most important knowledge a mother needs to have?
A. Exclusive breastfeeding for first six months of life, is the best gift you can give your baby.
